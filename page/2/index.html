About Dr. Nate Balfanz
Dr. Nate Balfanz (CA#PSY26707) is a clinical psychologist, mental health consultant,
and presenter licensed by the state of California.

Relocating from Los Angeles to Shanghai in the summer of 2014,
Dr. Nate brings a wealth of clinical and research expertise with
therapeutic specialties in treating children, young adults, and families.

Currently, Dr. Nate is employed as the Senior Clinical Psychologist at
American Medical Center (AMC) a multidisciplinary medical and mental health clinic
with an office conveniently located in the Puxi region of Shanghai.
Clinic services include child and adolescent-focused psychotherapy, adult psychotherapy, relational and
family support, parenting instruction, evidence-based treatment, as well as academic/occupational performance support.
